# 🎵 Music Folder

## Add Your Songs Here!

Place your music files (`.mp3`, `.wav`, `.ogg`) in this folder.

### Quick Setup:

1. **Add your music files here:**
   ```
   song1.mp3
   song2.mp3
   song3.mp3
   ```

2. **Update the playlist** in:
   `src/os/apps/MusicApp.jsx` (line ~14)

3. **Refresh browser** and enjoy!

### Example Files:
```
public/music/
├── song1.mp3        ← Your first song
├── song2.mp3        ← Your second song
└── song3.mp3        ← Your third song
```

### Supported Formats:
- ✅ `.mp3` (Recommended - works everywhere)
- ✅ `.wav` (High quality, large files)
- ✅ `.ogg` (Good compression)
- ✅ `.m4a` (AAC, limited support)
